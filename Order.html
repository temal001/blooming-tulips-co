Ready to Order? Here's What to Know

We deliver throughout Louisville and nearby areas. Standard delivery windows are 9 AM – 6 PM. You’ll receive a confirmation email after placing your order.

If you need a specific delivery time, mention it in the notes. We’ll try our best, but exact times aren’t guaranteed unless arranged beforehand.

For weddings, parties, or large events, please contact us 1–2 weeks in advance so we can prepare custom florals.

Please avoid placing same‑day orders without checking availability first. We want your flowers to arrive fresh and on time.
